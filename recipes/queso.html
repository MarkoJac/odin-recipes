Queso

Description

This queso recipe is low calorie and high protein without compromising on flavor. It is a great way to satisfy cravings and add protein to your meals. It is super easy to make and can be modified to your personal tastes. It is meal prep friendly and stores well in the fridge for up to 2 weeks!

Cottage cheese and low-fat skim cheese are great options here if you are in a calorie deficit. If you just want a high protein option and aren't worried about the calories cottage cheese can be mixed with your full fat cheese of choice. The secret ingredient here is sodium citrate, a flavorless cooking salt that causes the cheese to melt just like your favorite store bought queso options. You can make it yourself at home or simply buy it online ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of non-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 cup part skim cheddar cheese
  • 8 grams sodium citrate
  • 3 oz water
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Combine cottage and cheddar cheese in a medium sized pot with the water and sodium citrate
  2. Cook on medium heat stirring constantly until cheese sauce reaches a smooth consistency
  3. Add the seasonings and stir
  4. Add additional toppings like chilis or onions if desired
  5. If you prefer a runnier sauce add water 1 Tbsp at a time until desired consistency is reached
