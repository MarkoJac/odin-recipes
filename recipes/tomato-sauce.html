Tomato Sauce

Description

Great tomato sauce uses only a few simple ingredients and is infinitley better that what you get in a jar. Tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and basil are all you need to make a comforting delicious sauce for your next pasta dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 can san marzano tomatoes
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1 cup chopped basil
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp MSG
  • 2 pinches salt and pepper
  • Optional: 1/2 cup red wine

Directions

  1. Dump your tomatoes into a bowl and crush them with your hands. Remove any leaves or stems.
  2. Peel your garlic and finely mince, grate, or press it.
  3. In a sauce pan heat your olive oil over medium heat until shimmering.
  4. Gently fry the garlic until fragrant
  5. Add red wine to the pan and simmer for 2 minutes
  6. Add tomatoes to the pan and bring to a gentle simmer
  7. Lower heat to medium low and add seasonings and half the basil. Let cook on low for 30 minutes
  8. Add the rest of the basil when serving
