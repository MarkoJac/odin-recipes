Berry Smoothie

Description

This delicous frozen berry smoothie is high in fiber and protein and is a great source of antioxidants. If you are struggling to reach your fiber goals adding one of these to your day will get you 90% of the way there!

Made of simple ingredients and ready in minutes this this smoothie will change your life! The addition of frozen peas adds a nutty flavor while packing in nutrients, protein, and fiber

Ingrdients

  • 140g mixed frozen berries
  • 80g frozen peas
  • 1 tbsp psyllium husk powder
  • 1 scoop of your favorite protein powder
  • 12 oz water or milk of choice

Directions

  1. Add all the ingredients your blender and blend
  2. Enjoy!
